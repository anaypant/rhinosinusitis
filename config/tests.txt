These are the tests available to recommend from:

Imaging: CT Scan, MRI, Endoscopic Evaluation, Nasal Endoscopy
Allergy: Skin Prick Test, IgE Blood Test, 
Microbiological: Sinus Culture, Nasal Swab, 
Functional: Ciliary Function Tests, Saccharin Test
Biochemical and Inflammatory Markers: CRP, ESR, Eosinophil Count
Specialized Tests: Sweat Chloride Test, Genetic Testing, ASA (Aspirin Sensitivity Test)
Other Tests: Smell Identification Test, pH Testing, Pulmonary Function Test
